# Product Definition

## Initial Concept
Local AI-powered home battery optimization for solar homes.

## Target Users
- **Homeowners with Solar + Battery setups**: Seeking to minimize electricity bills through automated optimization.
- **Energy Enthusiasts**: Seeking detailed monitoring, transparency, and the ability to manually intervene or test scenarios.
- **Home Assistant Users**: Seeking a deeply integrated solution that works natively within their existing smart home ecosystem.

## Core Features
- **Smart Battery Optimization**: Employs Mixed-Integer Linear Programming (MILP) to minimize costs over a 48-hour horizon.
- **ML-based Forecasting**: Predicts solar production and household load patterns to inform optimization strategies.
- **Real-time Home Assistant Integration**: Bi-directional control and data synchronization with Home Assistant for seamless execution.

## Success Metrics
- **Cost Reduction**: Tangible percentage reduction in monthly electricity expenditures.
- **System Reliability**: High uptime and robust execution of inverter controls to ensure energy management is never compromised.

## User Experience Priorities
- **Live Data Visualization**: High-granularity charts and schedule visualizations for the 48-hour rolling horizon.
- **At-a-Glance Status**: Simplified health indicators and summaries of cost savings for quick assessment.

## Operational Philosophy
The system is designed for **High Automation with Monitoring Transparency**. While most users will prefer a "set and forget" automated optimization, the project prioritizes detailed monitoring over manual control. Power users retain the ability to tweak settings, but the primary interaction is observing the system's intelligent decision-making.
